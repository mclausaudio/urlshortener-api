Welcome to LilBit

URL shortening service created by Michael Claus

To use, simply send a POST request to:

thisURL/api/<< URL you would like masked or shortened >>

Here are some examples:

thisUrl/api/www.yahoo.com
thisUrl/api/www.google.com
thisUrl/api/www.espn.com

The URL you provide should be in a simple www.url.com(or .org or .net, whichever) format.
Do not include http:// or https://.
